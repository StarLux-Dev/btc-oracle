Model Methodology

Random Walk: Naive baseline — tomorrow's price = today's price.

ARIMA-like (drift): Computes mean log return + AR(1) component.

GARCH (vol-aware): Drift + conditional volatility band.

Mean Reversion: Pulls toward 30-day moving average.

Momentum (EMA): EMA(7) vs EMA(25) crossover signal.

Sentiment-Weighted: Drift adjusted by community sentiment + macro.

On-Chain Supply: Cycle position + supply squeeze adjustment.

Adaptive Ensemble: Weighted average — weights = inverse RMSE from backtests.
